About Me

Hi! I'm Jada Nelson, a passionate music enthusiast who enjoys exploring diverse musical landscapes. Welcome to my corner of the internet where I share discoveries, artist spotlights, and deep dives into the stories behind the music that moves me.

I believe that every song has a story, and every artist has something unique to offer. Through this blog, I hope to bridge the gap between listeners and the music that could change their lives. I want to uncover both emerging and established artists who push boundaries and create meaningful connections through their art. Whether you're looking for your next favorite artist or want to understand the deeper context behind a beloved song, you'll find something here that resonates with your musical soul.

My Musical Expertise

There are various aspects of music and the indistry that I appreciate!

  • Genre Exploration: R&B, indie, alternative, hip hop, folk, rock, classical, and pop
  • Music Production: Understanding recording techniques and how technology shapes sound
  • Music History: Tracing the evolution of genres and understanding cultural contexts
  • Artist Development: Following emerging artists and understanding career trajectories
  • Live Music: Covering concerts, festivals, and the importance of live performances
  • Music Business: Understanding streaming, record labels, and how artists navigate the industry

When I'm Not Listening to Music

Honestly, I'm almost always listening to music... But when I need a break from my headphones, I enjoy visiting cat cafes, vinyl shopping, and playing piano (I'm way better at listening than playing). I also love watching video essays and documentaries on music and that give insight into artists' creative processes.

Let's Connect

I love connecting with other music enthusiasts, musicians, and anyone who's passionate about discovering new sounds. Whether you want to recommend an artist I should check out, discuss a blog post, or just share your musical journey, don't hesitate to reach out to me through the contact page. Music is meant to be shared, and I'm always excited to hear about what's moving you!
